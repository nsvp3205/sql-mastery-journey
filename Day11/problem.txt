Fetch the user_id of users who liked videos from at least 2 different channels
but are subscribed to exactly 1 channel.

Conditions:
- reaction_type = 'LIKE'
- Join user_likes with video to access channel_id
- Group by user_id
- HAVING COUNT(DISTINCT video.channel_id) >= 2
- Use subquery on channel_user
- Subscription condition: COUNT(channel_id) = 1
- Sort in ascending order

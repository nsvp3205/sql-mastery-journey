Fetch the user_ids of users who liked videos from exactly 1 channel
in the year 2022 and have subscribed to at least 1 channel.

Conditions:
- Consider reaction_type = 'LIKE'
- Extract year from reacted_at
- "Exactly 1 channel" means COUNT(DISTINCT channel_id) = 1
- User must have at least one subscription in channel_user
- Return unique user_ids
- Sort results in ascending order

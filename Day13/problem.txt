Fetch the user_id of users who:
- Liked at least 2 videos in 2023
- And liked videos from exactly 1 channel in 2023

Conditions:
- reaction_type = 'LIKE'
- Extract year from reacted_at
- Join user_likes with video to access channel_id
- Group by user_id
- HAVING COUNT(video_id) >= 2
- HAVING COUNT(DISTINCT video.channel_id) = 1
- Sort results in ascending order

Fetch the user_ids of users who liked videos from at least 2 different channels
in the year 2021 but have NOT subscribed to any channel.

Conditions:
- Consider reaction_type = 'LIKE'
- Extract year from reacted_at
- "At least 2 different channels" means COUNT(DISTINCT channel_id) >= 2
- Exclude users who appear in channel_user
- Return unique user_ids
- Sort in ascending order

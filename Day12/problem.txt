Fetch the user_id of users who:
- Liked at least 3 videos in 2022
- And are subscribed to at least 2 channels

Conditions:
- reaction_type = 'LIKE'
- Extract year from reacted_at
- Group by user_id on likes side
- HAVING COUNT(video_id) >= 3
- Use subquery on channel_user
- GROUP BY user_id on subscription side
- HAVING COUNT(channel_id) >= 2
- Sort results in ascending order

The objective is to analyze the financial performance of movies in a dataset. 
For each movie, we need to calculate the Net Profit (Collection minus Budget) and determine the applicable Tax Amount based on a progressive scale.

The tax rates are defined as follows:

10% Tax: For profits up to 100 Cr.

15% Tax: For profits between 100 Cr and 500 Cr (inclusive).

18% Tax: For profits exceeding 500 Cr.
